Short Description of the Task Done in Week 4:

For Week 4, we worked on the Week4_v2 results and the hierarchical_cluster_summarizer.py script. We used the ARC, ACDC, and LIMBO clustering outputs and converted them into file-level clusters for the Hadoop YARN capacity scheduler code.

After that, we generated file-level summaries first and then used those summaries to create cluster-level architectural summaries. The final outputs were saved as ARC_results.csv, ACDC_results.csv, and LIMBO_results.csv inside the Week4_v2 folder.

We also saved the prompts, converted clusters, file summaries, and raw LLM outputs inside Week4_v2/hierarchical_outputs, so the process can be checked and repeated. This helped us understand and compare the architectural meaning of the clusters produced by the different algorithms.

This week we also worked on the report and prepared the presentation, including the explanation of our workflow, results, and final observations.
