**Key Functionality:**
This Java class, `QueueManagementDynamicEditPolicy`, implements a scheduling policy for dynamic queue management in Apache Hadoop YARN (Yet Another Resource Negotiator). It enables auto-creation of child queues under managed parent queues.

**Core Logic:**

1. Initialization: Retrieves managed parent queues and configures monitoring interval.
2. `editSchedule()`: Periodically (based on `monitoringInterval`) reinitializes queues and manages auto-created leaf queues.
3. `manageAutoCreatedLeafQueues()`: Iterates through managed parent queues, applying auto-created queue management policies to each, and triggers scheduler updates for changes.
