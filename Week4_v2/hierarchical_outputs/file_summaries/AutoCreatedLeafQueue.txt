**Key Functionality:**
Manages auto-created leaf queues in a YARN (Yet Another Resource Negotiator) Capacity Scheduler, handling queue configuration, capacity updates, and validation.

**Core Logic:**

* Initializes and reinitializes queue configurations based on parent queues and templates
* Updates queue capacities to zero to prevent resource overcommitment
* Merges capacities from templates, considering absolute resource configurations and node labels
* Validates capacity demands and queue configurations to ensure correctness
